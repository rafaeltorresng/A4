## Quick orientation
- Brand is **Brazilian (pt-BR)** — traffic-accident compensation services. Professional + empathetic + descomplicado.
- Core palette: `#1E4FA1` (primary blue) · `#0F2E63` (deep navy) · `#FFC431` (yellow accent) · `#25D366` (WhatsApp green CTA).
- Fonts: **Poppins** (display, logo, weights 500/700/800) + **DM Sans** (body).
- Icons: bespoke outline SVGs in `landing/primitives.jsx` — stroke 1.5-1.6, always `#1E4FA1`. Don't substitute with Lucide.
- No emoji ever. No gradients-as-hero (solid navy overlays on photography). No left-border-accent cards.
- Copy pattern: 2-line H2 with the second line in brand color. Sentence case. "você" (informal).
